[**Content Manager**][0] is a free suite of Joomla extensions that allows you to **access control** who can edit articles from the Joomla frontend.  Version 1.0.4 Beta 1 is now available for download.  Combined several extensions from [**jxtended.com**][1], **Content Manager** allows you to create new user groups and limit users to create or modify articles on a per category basis.  For more information see the [**Content Manager**][0] page on The Art of Joomla web site.
