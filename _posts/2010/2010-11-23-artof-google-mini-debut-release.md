**ArtofGoogle Mini** is a component to allow seamless integration with a Google Mini appliance and your Joomla 1.5 web site. ArtofGoogle Mini polls your appliance directly and displays the results within the Joomla page so you can maintain a consistent user experience for both your content and search. For more information see [http://www.theartofjoomla.com/extensions/artof-google-mini.html][0].
