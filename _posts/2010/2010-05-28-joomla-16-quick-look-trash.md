This is a quick look at the new trash management features in Joomla 1.6\.

[![screenshot](/images/2010/joomla_articles_published.jpg) ][3]

Just like in Joomla 1.5, you can send content to the trash by selecting from the list and clicking the _Trash_ button in the toolbar.  In Joomla 1.6 this has been expanded to include all content, not just articles and menus.

[![screenshot](/images/2010/joomla_articles_trashed.jpg) ][4]

To permenantly remove content from the trash, you need to set the state filter to "Trash".  The _Trash_ button in the toolbar will then change to _Empty Trash_.  From here you can select content in the trash and then clicking the _Empty Trash_ button will delete them forever.  This provides a reasonable degree of safety against accidently deleting your content.
